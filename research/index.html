{{ page.title }}

My primary research interest is the provable security analysis of symmetric-key cryptographic modes using combinatorial techniques. My doctoral research focused on provable security against a classical adversary. At Inria, I started exploring the exciting new field of provable security against a quantum adversary, as part of the ERC project QUASYModo. At present I am studying proof frameworks for adapting classical counting techniques in post-quantum security proofs.

My complete academic CV (last updated in June 2023) can be found here.
