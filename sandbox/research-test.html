Research

Provable Security of Symmetric-Key Cryptography

My primary interest is the provable security analysis of symmetric-key cryptographic modes using combinatorial techniques. My doctoral work concerned security against a classical adversary; at Inria I began studying security against a quantum adversary, and I now study proof frameworks that adapt classical counting techniques to the post-quantum setting. My publications are also listed on DBLP.
