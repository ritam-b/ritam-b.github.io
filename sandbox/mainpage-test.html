Ritam Bhaumik

Symmetric-Key & Post-Quantum Cryptography

Cryptography Research Center · Technology Innovation Institute · Abu Dhabi

I am a Senior Researcher at the Cryptography Research Center in the Technology Innovation Institute (TII), Abu Dhabi, where I study the provable security of symmetric-key cryptographic modes — classical and post-quantum — using combinatorial techniques.

I completed my PhD in Computer Science at the Indian Statistical Institute, Kolkata, in December 2019. Since then I have held appointments at Inria Paris (COSMIQ), the LASEC at EPFL, and the University of Luxembourg (SnT), joining TII in April 2024. My complete curriculum vitæ is available here.

I always look forward to interacting with motivated students and young researchers. If you have something on your mind that you think might interest me, feel free to drop me a mail.
