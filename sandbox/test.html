{{ page.title }}

My primary research interest is the provable security analysis of symmetric-key cryptographic modes using combinatorial techniques. My doctoral research focused on the provable security against a classical adversary. At present, I'm exploring the exciting new field of provable security against a quantum adversary, as part of the ERC project QUASYModo.

My complete academic CV can be found here.
